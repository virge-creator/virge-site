## How it works

Let's say you sell musical instruments and you're adding a **Selmer Mark VI** to your catalog. Normally, you'd spend 30–60 minutes researching the product, writing a description, finding specs, sourcing images, and adding metadata.

With our content enrichment pipeline, you provide:

```

Category: Saxophones (optional)
```

And within minutes, you get back:

- **A rich product description** — engaging, SEO-optimized copy that tells the story of the instrument
- **Technical specifications** — structured data: material, key, origin, production years, finish
- **Curated images** — AI-selected and generated product imagery
- **Tags & metadata** — categories, search tags, and SEO fields
- **Multiple languages** — Dutch, English, German, French — whatever your markets need

Our pipeline combines multiple AI models and enrichment sources:

1. **Large Language Models** generate natural, accurate product descriptions based on comprehensive product knowledge

4. **SEO optimization** ensures your content ranks — with proper meta tags, structured data, and keyword placement

The result is content that reads like it was written by a specialist — because the AI was trained on specialist knowledge.
